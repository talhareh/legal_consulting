Our Specialized Services

We provide comprehensive financial recovery solutions tailored to the unique needs of businesses and individuals in Dubai and across the UAE.

Corporate Debt Recovery

Specialized in recovering outstanding debts for businesses of all sizes. We handle B2B disputes, contract breaches, and unpaid invoices with a strategic approach that prioritizes your business relationships while ensuring maximum recovery.

Banking & Financial Claims

Expert representation in banking disputes, loan defaults, and financial institution claims. We work with banks, financial institutions, and clients to resolve complex financial matters including mortgage disputes and credit card debts.

Real Estate Recovery

Comprehensive services for property-related financial disputes including developer defaults, rental disputes, property investment recovery, and construction payment claims across Dubai's real estate market.

Legal Judgment Enforcement

Professional enforcement of court judgments and arbitration awards. We ensure that winning a case translates into actual recovery through strategic asset tracing and execution procedures.

Asset Tracing & Investigation

Advanced asset tracing services to locate hidden or transferred assets. Our investigative team uses cutting-edge techniques and legal tools to uncover assets and build strong cases for recovery.

Negotiation & Settlement

Expert negotiation services to reach favorable settlements without lengthy litigation. Our skilled negotiators work to recover your funds through strategic dialogue and mediation.

International Recovery

Cross-border financial recovery services for international disputes. We coordinate with legal partners globally to recover assets and debts across multiple jurisdictions.

Insolvency & Restructuring

Strategic guidance during insolvency proceedings to maximize creditor recovery. We represent creditors in bankruptcy cases and assist with debt restructuring for optimal outcomes.

Consumer Financial Recovery

Dedicated services for individuals facing financial disputes including credit card defaults, personal loan recovery, and bounced cheque cases with compassionate yet effective representation.
